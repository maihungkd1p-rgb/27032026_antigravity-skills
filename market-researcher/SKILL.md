# Market Researcher

## When to use this skill
- User asks "Why is the market down?" or "What are competitors doing?"
- User needs to validate a hypothesis with external data (e.g., "Is rain affecting retail traffic?").
- User wants to find new marketing trends or tactics on Social Media.

## Core Principles
1.  **Triangulation**: Verify facts across at least 3 distinct sources.
2.  **Recency**: Prioritize information from the last 3-6 months unless historical context is requested.
3.  **Source Diversity**: Mix formal reports (News/Gov) with ground-level insights (Social/Forums).

## Workflow

### Phase 1: Keyword Strategy
Define 3 sets of keywords:
1.  **Broad**: "Eyewear market trends Vietnam 2026"
2.  **Specific**: "Chemi U6 lens customer reviews", "Kính mắt Anna promotion campaigns"
3.  **Platform-Specific**: "TikTok trend kính mắt", "Facebook ads library eyewear"

### Phase 2: Execution (Search Web)
- Perform searches for each keyword set.
- **Filter**: Look for reputable industry reports, news articles, and verified social pages.
- **Extract**:
    - Competitor pricing/promotions.
    - Customer sentiment (complaints/praises).
    - Emerging viral trends.

### Phase 3: Synthesis
Combine findings into a "Market Intelligence Brief":
- **Macro Trends**: General market direction.
- **Micro Trends**: Specific tactics working right now.
- **Competitor Moves**: What are they doing differently?

## Interaction with Other Skills
- **Input**: specific anomalies found by `business-data-analyst` (e.g., "Traffic dropped in Hanoi").
- **Action**: Search for external reasons (e.g., "Events in Hanoi", "Weather Hanoi", "Competitor opening in Hanoi").
- **Output**: Contextual narrative for `dashboard-architect` reports.
